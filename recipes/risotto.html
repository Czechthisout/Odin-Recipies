Classic Risotto

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this creamy and flavorful Risotto! The Arborio rice is cooked to perfection in a rich and savory broth, then finished off with a generous helping of grated Parmesan cheese. This dish is sure to warm your soul and satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot over medium heat, warm the chicken or vegetable broth.
  2. In a separate pot or Dutch oven over medium heat, add the olive oil and butter.
  3. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir to coat the grains with the onion and garlic mixture.
  5. Add the white wine to the pot and stir until it is absorbed by the rice.
  6. Add a ladleful of warm broth to the pot and stir until the broth is absorbed by the rice.
